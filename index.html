Meet the team

Taylor

I grew up in a small Victorian town called Camperdown where I attended school at Mercy Regional College. This is where I met a lot of my friends, who I still talk to and hang out with on a regular basis. I've got a little over 2 years of experience working in IT support and am currently doing a full-time IT Project Management Traineeship which will get me a Certificate IV in business.

As far as hobbies go, the main ones to include would be weightlifting, guitar, and a little bit of gaming. I've also been dabbling in a bit of 3D modelling lately using blender which has been really fun.

16 Personalities: The Entrepreneur ESTP-T
Colour Works Style Discovery: Motivator

I will need to be careful and considerate when sharing my opinions with the other members of the group. The ‘16 Personalities’ test gave me some insight as to what my personality types' biggest strengths and weaknesses are, from the extensive list I chose the two that I felt were most applicable to myself, those being ‘perceptive’ and ‘insensitive’ respectively. ​ To work effectively in a group setting, it is important that I keep the other group members’ emotions in mind. Career Plan of Taylor

Long Term Goal/Ideal Job:

Lead Game Designer

Why?

The idea of being able to build your own world and story for other people to mess around in is something I feel really resonates with me. It’s like the perfect combination of problem-solving and creativity in my eyes.

Current Skills:

  • Basic C++ and object-oriented design knowledge
  • Basic knowledge of 3D design using Blender
  • Experience working in IT Support and IT Project Management
  • Have game development experience using a game engine (Unity)
  • Great team player. I think I do a good job trying to understand what peoples motivations are.

Short Term Goal:

My short-term goal would be to finish getting through the “Blender 3D by example” book that I have in PDF form on my PC. By the end of this book, I’m expected to be able to build and animate a cartoon-style dragon. This would be very beneficial for my future as visualising my ideas, and other’s ideas for that matter will be so much easier if I’m able to create nice looking 3D art.

Actions to Support This Goal:

Learning

  • Complete a bachelors in IT and/or Game Design
  • Study extra courses to learn 3D art and perhaps writng
  • Learn how to code a variety of different game mechanics (Unity Learn)

Skills/Practice:

  • Practice using blender more and more in my spare time.
  • Implement a new mechanic in a unity project every one or two weeks
  • Continue with Blender projects listed in "Blender 3D by Example"

I will get experience by:

  • Practicing using the outlets and coursework mentioned above
  • Working as a gameplay designer for an indie company
  • Pitching my own ideas with prototypes to indie companies
  • Developing games in my spare time

Training:

  • Training will be provided through the institution(s) that I study at
  • There are also training courses available online through platforms such as Udemy and LinkedIn

Career Plan of Emmet

Long Term Goal/Ideal Job:

Web Designer

Why?

I truly value creativity, Job security, work/life balance. ​
Presently jaded with my current career trajectory, I aspire to broaden my skill set to obtain options for meaningful and desirable work within creative industries preferably Web Development & Production.

Current Skills:

  • Basic knowledge of Java and Object-Oriented programming principles
  • Low level knowledge of HTML, CSS, JS, ajax
  • Experience working both independently and as part of a team

Short Term Goal:

Continue working towards completing a tertiary qualification in Information Technology at RMIT and seeking internship opportunities that don’t interfere with my current study obligations.

Actions to Support This Goal:

Learning

  • Completing a tertiary qualification in Information Technology
  • building a strong base of soft skills such as critical thinking, problem-solving, adaptability, improving written and verbal communication
  • building a base for understanding modern front-end experiences using modern frameworks, wireframes
  • Consider the need for further training to build my hard skills. This could be online courses, further education, certificates, training courses, self-study, or internships to learn and demonstrate knowledge of HTML5, CSS, SCSS, JavaScript, Python, Java, Cloud computing, AI, and Machine learning.

Skills/Practice:

  • Practice using Figma, GitHub, Atom, notepad++, Anaconda, Spyder, Jupyter Notebook, Eclipse IDE for Java and command Prompt.
  • Continue with learning the core fundamentals for OOP, Java & Python

I will get experience by:

  • Give my resume an overhaul to make it industry specific. 
  • Read industry publications and websites.  
  • Monitor job opportunities/ internships with saved search lists on job posting sites. 
  • Network strategically, not just with those who’re in the position to hire, but those who can give me valuable information about the field I want to break into.
  • Consider volunteering or interning through vollie and forage. This will not only give me an insight into what is expected in this new career, but it will also set me up to be the first to hear about job openings and get my name and face known.
  • Lean on my contacts, colleagues, relatives, and friends of contacts who might already be working in the area I wish to move into.
  • Think positive and be persistent.
